Impact of Soylent Consumption on Humans

As students carry out their busy lifestyles, many are turning to inexpensive and convenient drink based meal-alternatives, such as Soylent, to supplement or replace their regular diets. We crowdfunded $6,405 to start a project to track the composition of participant's microbiome before, during, and after Soylent use to more holistically understand Soylent's impact on microbiome health.

Software Development

I've always been interested in visual design, and I've ventured through nearly every aspect of digital art. From filmmaking to photography, 3d modeling, motion graphics, animation and vfx, I've had to learn countless programs and plugins. I've always very much been project based when it comes to learning software, so I would start by thinking of something I wanted to create, and then using whatever tools were available for me to get it done.

My interests shifted over time as I explored new frontiers, using my growing base of knowledge sourced from whatever I could figure out or find on the internet. It's always been challenging, but tech, like art, is never easy, and when you combine the two the problems with each are compounded. But so is the reward, I always did it because I found it more fulfilling because of how challenging it was, and how amazing the result felt.

Throughout my experiences across mediums, I've learned the power of user experience, and the depth and variety of thoughts and emotions that you can provoke through visual engagement. These reactions are what keep me coming back to visual design, so that I may create and inspire, turning ideas into reality.
